OIL AND GAS



AI technology is also doubtlessly a new shining star that draws attention from researchers who devoted themselves to it. The applications of artificial intelligence in oilfield development a hint of the future trend of this exciting technology in the oil and gas industry. The application of AI in issues of oilfield development including oilfield production dynamic prediction, developing plan optimization, residual oil identification, fracture identification, and enhanced oil recovery are specifically investigated and summarized. The current situation of the application of AI in oilfield development is concluded, and suggestions and potential directions of future work AI application in oil and gas development are being explored.

Original & unpublished research papers are invited for presentation in the following areas, but not restricted to.

  • Knowledge-Based Systems
  • ML in Petroleum Industry
  • Seismic Interpretation
  • Petroleum Exploration
  • Pattern Recognition
  • Intelligent reservoir characterization
  • Intelligent Geological Modelling
  • Direct Hydrocarbon Prediction
