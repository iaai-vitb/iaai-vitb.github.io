MATERIAL SCIENCE



Evaluation of Materials performances using artificial intelligence (AI) tools such as machine learning, deep learning, and various optimization techniques is under thorough research and progressive development has been achieved. The applicability of AI in material design is still under the developmental phase with constant efforts being put in the domain. The key challenges in selecting effective descriptors for materials and algorithms during AI design. Uncertain quantification in AI-based predictions for material properties and issues related to building infrastructure for disseminating AI knowledge is of immense importance for making AI-based investigation of materials successful.

Original & unpublished research papers are invited for presentation in the following areas, but not restricted to.

  • Data Analysis
  • DataBase Screening for Materials
  • Property Prediction
  • Synthetic routes identification
  • Material Modelling
  • Autonomous Experimentation Systems
