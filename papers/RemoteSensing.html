REMOTE SENSING



Remote sensing is a unique source for the collection of Big Data which is generally produced by platforms like satellites, manned-aircrafts, ground stations, etc. Developments in the field of AI enabled us to process, manage and analyze such large-scale data with the ultimate goal being the promotion of sustainable development of machine learning algorithms for efficient analysis of remotely sensed big data.

Original & unpublished research papers are invited for presentation in the following areas, but not restricted to.

  • Land cover mapping
  • Object-based image analysis
  • Quantitative modelling
  • Biophysical/biochemical parameter retrieval
  • Yield estimation
  • Ecological assessment
