ROBOTICS



The next big thing is transferring capabilities to robots to ease the various process and automate most work to effectively reduce operational time and increase production as well as reducing latencies at a human level. Automation is the new future and large-scale applications are solely dependent on automated machines to guide and assist them. The incorporation of AI to develop smart assistance and continuously iterate and improve upon the various possibilities in the domain is an extensive field of research and is being rapidly evolving to make sustainable and significant advancements for the next classes of humanoid, mobile robots guided by AI possibilities.

Original & unpublished research papers are invited for presentation in the following areas, but not restricted to.

  • Aerial and Underwater Robotics
  • Opto-Mechatronics
  • Human-Robot Interfaces
  • Humanoid Robots
  • Parallel Robotics
  • Probabilistic Robotics
  • Robust and Process Control
  • Visual Robotics
  • Robot Calibration
  • Vision, Recognition and Reconstruction
