EDUCATION 4.0



Education 4.0 will be a digital revolution that will affect many stakeholders, including educators as well as students. It enables a teacher to teach a particular student, rather than a whole class using tools and techniques which promote a personalized learning goal and that leads to a greater learning outcome for students. AI can play an essential role in education 4.0 by pinpointing new factors such as the performance of students and implementing personalized learning using learning analytics and predictive modeling. As the saying goes, “A calm mind is the ultimate weapon”, AI will be used to improve support for a student’s mental health and wellbeing.

Original & unpublished research papers are invited for presentation in the following areas, but not restricted to.

  • Transformed Teaching
  • Personalized Adaptive Learning
  • Assessment Re-imagined
  • Intelligent Digital and physical Estates
